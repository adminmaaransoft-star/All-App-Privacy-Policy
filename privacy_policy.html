EZY-Drive Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Maaransoft ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the EZY-Drive mobile application (the "App"), a driver application for the EZY-Drive on-demand transportation platform.

Summary — what we collect and why
  • Location (foreground and background) — to accept trip requests, navigate to pickups, calculate fares, and share your location with the passenger during an active trip.
  • Phone number — for OTP-based login and driver identification.
  • Profile photo and documents (driving licence, PAN card, etc.) — for driver verification and regulatory compliance.
  • Device information — for security, fraud prevention, and diagnostics.
  • Payment information — processed by Razorpay for wallet top-ups; we do not store card or UPI credentials on our servers.
  • Notification token — to send you trip alerts and status updates.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Information

With your permission, we collect precise location data from your device using GPS and network location services. The App collects location:

  • While in use (foreground) — continuously while you are logged in and marked online, so we can allocate trip requests and display your position on the map.
  • In the background — while the App is minimized or the screen is off, so we can continue tracking your position for allocation, safety, and billing purposes even when you are not actively looking at the App.

Location data is transmitted to our servers approximately every two minutes while you are online. You can stop this collection at any time by going offline in the App, revoking location permission in your device settings, or logging out.

1.2 Account Information

  • Mobile number (used as your account identifier and for OTP verification)
  • Name, address, and profile details you provide during registration
  • Driving licence number, PAN card, and other identity documents you upload
  • Vehicle information (type, classification, transmission)
  • Language preferences and driver skills you configure

1.3 Device Information

  • Android device identifier (ANDROID_ID)
  • Device manufacturer, model, and Android OS version
  • App version and installation identifier
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications
  • Network connectivity status

1.4 Trip and Usage Information

  • Trips you accept, complete, or cancel, including pickup and drop-off points and timestamps
  • Distance travelled, fare calculated, and payment method
  • Ratings and reviews from passengers
  • Wallet balance and recharge history

1.5 Financial Information

Wallet recharges and top-ups are processed by Razorpay Software Private Limited, a third-party payment gateway. Card numbers, UPI IDs, bank account details, and CVVs are entered directly into Razorpay's secure interface and are not stored on our servers or retained by the App. We receive only the transaction status, transaction identifier, and amount.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and manage your driver account
  • To match you with nearby trip requests
  • To calculate trip distance, fares, and applicable tolls
  • To share your live location with the assigned passenger for their safety and pickup coordination
  • To verify your identity and documents for regulatory compliance
  • To process wallet payments and settlement
  • To send trip alerts, promotional notifications, and service updates
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • To detect fraud, prevent abuse, and enforce our terms
  • To improve the App's features, stability, and performance

4. How We Share Your Information

We share information only as described below. We do not sell your personal information.

  • Passengers — your first name, vehicle details, and live location are shared with the assigned passenger during an active trip.
  • Our servers and hosting providers — data is stored on secure servers operated on our behalf.
  • Razorpay — payment details are sent directly to Razorpay for processing. See Razorpay's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase (Cloud Messaging, Installations) — used to deliver push notifications. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Google Maps & Places — used to display maps, calculate routes, and resolve addresses. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Regulatory and law enforcement authorities — where required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of drivers, passengers, or the public.
  • In a business transfer — if EZY-Drive is acquired or merged, information may be transferred to the successor entity subject to this Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information and trip history for as long as your driver account is active, and for a reasonable period after deactivation to comply with tax, accounting, safety, and dispute-resolution obligations (typically up to 7 years for financial records, as required by Indian law). Location data older than 90 days is aggregated or anonymized where practical.

6. Deleting Your Account and Data

You may request deletion of your driver account and associated personal data at any time by:

  • Sending an email to admin.maaransoft@gmail.com with the subject line "EZY-Drive Account Deletion Request", from the email address linked to your account, and including your registered mobile number.

We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Records we are legally required to keep (such as completed-trip financial records) will be retained for the minimum period required by law, isolated from active systems, and then permanently deleted.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted-at-rest storage of session credentials on the device (AndroidX Security Crypto), TLS in transit, and access controls on server-side data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

EZY-Drive is not intended for use by children. Drivers must hold a valid driving licence, which requires the user to be an adult. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your account and data (see Section 6)
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at admin.maaransoft@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be notified to you within the App or by email. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
